CuriosityCore

AI for all.

An accessible platform for creating decentralized, modular, self-learning AI's

Accessible. Interact with AI's using natural language or graphical interface.
Self-Learning. AI's Learn any task and get better over time.
Decentralized. Runs locally on any device/hardware using any data.
Clustered. Can share data and computing power across a network.
Modular. Connect or remove modules to easily implement or repurpose.

We are showcasing this technology by building a decentralized organization, 
CORE, managed by a CuriosityCore built AI and controlled by it's human stakeholders.
